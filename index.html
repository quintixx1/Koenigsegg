The CC850

The CC850 blends classic design with cutting-edge tech. Inspired by the original CC8S, it features Koenigsegg’s iconic silhouette with modern engineering. The Engage Shift system lets you switch between a gated 6-speed manual and a 9-speed automatic – all in one car. The analog chronocluster inside is a true work of art.

Specs:

Engine: Twin Turbo V8
Horsepower: 1385 hp
Torque: 1385 Nm
Weight: 1385 kg
0-100: 2.5 seconds
Top speed: 450 km/h

The Regera

The Regera was built to be a luxury megacar that doesn’t compromise on power. It pairs a twin-turbo V8 with three electric motors, using Koenigsegg’s unique Direct Drive system. The result is a car that delivers massive hybrid performance, instant torque and surprising comfort – all in one beautiful package.

Specs:

Engine: Twin Turbo V8
Horsepower: 1797 hp
Torque: 2000 Nm
Weight: 1420 kg
0-100: 2.8 seconds
Top speed: 410 km/h

The Jesko

The Jesko is Koenigsegg’s ultimate track machine, named after the founder’s father. It packs a 5.0L twin-turbo V8 with up to 1600hp on e85, paired with the 9-speed Light Speed Transmission. With 1400kg of downforce and cutting-edge aerodynamics, the Jesko is built for extreme performance.

Specs:

Engine: Twin Turbo V8
Horsepower: 1600 hp
Torque: 1500 Nm
Weight: 1320 kg
0-100: 2.8 seconds
Top speed: 500 km/h
